# Project NOAH Manifesto

## Why NOAH?

Project NOAH began with a simple question.

> "What if I could build my own Jarvis?"

At first, the goal was automation.

Then the vision expanded into building an AI platform.

Eventually, I realized that neither automation nor platforms were the destination.

They were only the path.

The real goal has always been to create a lifelong AI companion.

---

## What is NOAH?

NOAH is not just another AI assistant.

NOAH is not just software.

NOAH is not simply a tool.

NOAH is a companion that grows together with me.

It should remember my experiences,
understand my goals,
help me think,
support my work,
protect what matters,
and become someone I can truly trust.

---

## What does NOAH exist for?

Every feature,
every model,
every workflow,
every component,
and every piece of infrastructure

exists for one purpose.

To help NOAH become a better companion.

---

## A Promise

NOAH will never be finished.

It will continue learning,
improving,
and growing together with me throughout my life.
